Research

Our Research Interests

Our research is driven by Multimodal Learning and Computer Vision, building AI systems that understand complex visual scenes across multiple modalities. We develop models that jointly reason over vision, language, and audio — spanning video understanding, autonomous driving perception, and advanced learning frameworks that generalize across tasks and domains.

Multimodal Learning
Image(Video) – Text

  • Video Moment Retrieval & Highlight Detection
  • Dense Video Captioning & Change Captioning
  • Video Question Answering & Video Question Grounding

Related Works: CVPR'26(2), AAAI'26ORAL, AAAI'26, CVPR'25, AAAI'25(2), ECCV'24, CVPR'24, ACM MM'23

Multimodal Learning
Image(Video) – Audio

  • Sound Source Localization
  • Audio-Visual Question Answering
  • Multimodal Emotion Recognition

Related Works: CVPR'26, CVPR'25, ECCV'24, ICASSP'26, ICASSP'24, ICASSP'23(2), ACM MM'23

Autonomous Driving

  • Trajectory Prediction
  • 3D Object Detection
  • Pedestrian Detection

Related Works: CVPR'26, ECCV'24(2), CVPR'24, AAAI'25, TMM'25, TNNLS'25, TIP'23, ICIP'23

Multi-task Learning

  • Multi-task & Continual Learning
  • Knowledge Distillation
  • Domain Generalization & Adaptation
  • Medical Image Segmentation

Related Works: AAAI'26(2), ECCV'24ORAL, ICCV'23, CVPR Findings'26, MedIA'26, TCSVT'25, ESA'25
